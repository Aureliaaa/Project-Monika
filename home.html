About the mod

Project Monika came to be when we asked the question, "What if the literature club never existed?" and with that Project Monika was born. The purpose of this mod is to expand on the story side of Doki Doki Literature Club! which we did not get to see much of.


Meet the team!

We currently only have a small team of three actively working on this mod and would greatly apreciate any help we could get! If you or someone you know would like to join the team please ask them to contact us!
